Thank you for taking part in my user study for Clouds in the Cloud!

You will be presented with a set of video pairs. Each pair consits of a experimental and reference video, but the order is random. The videos are played automatically one after the other.

After you have seen both of each pair, you will be asked which one you prefer by picking a number on a scale from -2 to 2.

Of course you can pick any number in between:

  • -2 = Strong tendency towards 1st video
  • -1 = Slight tendency towards 1st video
  • 0 = No tendency
  • 1 = Slight tendency towards 2nd video
  • 2 = Strong tendency towards 2nd video
Your input just have to be your picked number.

The duration of the user study is approx. 15-18 minutes. Please click on the button right handside to proceed to the first pair.
